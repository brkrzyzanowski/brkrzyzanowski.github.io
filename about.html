I compose, record, and produce music. I do voice overs.  I’m a sound designer.  Help me help you.  Do you have a film, cartoon, podcast, commercial, album, gentle heart, or even a dog with good taste in music? In need of some dulcet tones?  Let’s work together!

I began strumming a guitar at the tender age of 16 and haven’t put it down yet (even to go to the bathroom).  I find inspiration from a broad range of styles including classical, orchestral, chip tunes, rock, hip hop, construction work, and bangin’ on a trash can.
